What to Expect

Before & after your tan

  1. 01
    24 hours before

    Exfoliate and shave. Skip lotion, oil, deodorant, and perfume on tan day.

  2. 02
    The day of

    Wear loose, dark clothing. Bring flip-flops. Your session takes about 20 minutes.

  3. 03
    After your tan

    Wait 8 hours (or your Rapid Tan rinse time) before showering. Pat dry, moisturize daily, and avoid long soaks.

  4. 04
    Make it last

    Most clients get 7–10 days with proper care. Re-book when your color starts to fade.
